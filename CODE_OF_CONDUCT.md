# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience level, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and welcoming.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Accept responsibility, apologize for mistakes, and learn from them.
- Respect privacy, especially when examples could contain financial data.

Harassment, intimidation, discriminatory language, personal attacks, and publishing another person's private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to **tolstoyjustin@gmail.com**. Reports will be reviewed fairly and confidentially. Maintainers may remove contributions or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project publicly.
